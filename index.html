Hello. I'm Toby Hodges.

Why create software?

I build software because I believe in people's ability to use it as a tool to help plan, design, and create better lives for people.

To increase quality of life for all.

Or it's only to provide for me and my family and anything else I tell myself is a coping mechanism for the suffering incurred by the system we live in. There's probably some truth to that.


I work as a senior software engineer for Autodesk in Portland, Oregon. Before that, I worked as a software engineer for Kaiser Permanente.

There are a few things that I consider my passions, though my friends and family would tell you that I'm passionate about everything. My penultimate desire is to create a better world. Please, ask me about it. It is my belief that to do so, we must intentionally plan, design, and make spaces that are equitable for people of every background and that provide means of living sustainably. I want to build software that supports this effort, which is why I'm working to attain a Graduate Certificate in Urban Design from Portland State University. It's why I have spent many days reading and attending lectures and workshops to hear from both the dominant voices and those largely unheard.
